# **App Name**: Forever Us

## Core Features:

- Date Timer: Calculate and display the duration (days, hours, seconds) since February 11th, 2025, indicating the length of the relationship.
- Days of Love Calculation: Display the number of days since March 17th, 2024 (when you started loving her) and compare it to the days since February 11th, 2025, showing that your love period is longer.
- Memory Game: Interactive memory game with 5 questions about significant moments in the relationship, each with multiple-choice answers and a sweet after-click message.
- Interactive Timeline: A visual timeline showcasing 10 key moments with photos and descriptive text for each milestone.
- Personalized Message: A heartfelt message expressing the depth of your feelings and emphasizing her importance in your life.
- Photo Upload: Allow photo upload for populating the time line

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) to symbolize love, thoughtfulness, and the beauty of a budding romance.
- Background color: Pale cream (#F5F5DC) to create a warm and inviting atmosphere.
- Accent color: Dusty rose (#D8BFD8) to highlight key elements and add a touch of romance.
- Body font: 'Literata', a transitional serif with a literary, vintage, slightly formal feel; to be used in the paragraphs and message section
- Headline font: 'Belleza', A humanist sans-serif that with a personality aligned to fashion, art, and design; suitable for headlines and short chunks of text, and will complement the body font
- Use elegant and simple icons related to love and memories. Keep them consistent and minimal.
- Use a clean and responsive layout. Prioritize readability and easy navigation.
- Subtle, romantic animations on the timer and during the memory game. Add fade-in effects for the timeline.
